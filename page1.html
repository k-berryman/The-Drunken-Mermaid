The Siren’s Secret Menu & Hidden Gems

Author

Laurie

Published

August 27, 2025

If you’ve found your way to The Drunken Mermaid, you’ve already mastered the first rule of the coast: follow the sound of the shaker. But while our locals live for the classics, there’s a whole world beneath the surface that doesn't always make it to the chalkboard.

Since 2010, our mother-daughter founders have been tucking away "off-book" recipes inspired by a decade of sunsets and shipwrecks. Today, we’re letting you in on the tide’s best-kept secrets.

1. The "Deep Sea" Drift
You won’t find this on the printed menu. It’s a moody, botanical twist on a traditional punch. We take our house white rum, muddle it with fresh blackberries, and top it with a splash of sparkling ginger beer. It’s dark, fizzy, and just a little bit dangerous—exactly like the trench it’s named after.

2. The "Sandbar" Slider
Not all treasures are liquid. Ask your server for the Sandbar Style on our classic fish tacos. It adds a spicy, house-made mango-habanero slaw that provides just enough heat to make you reach for your next drink. It’s the ultimate "if you know, you know" move for the true spice-seekers.

3. The "Pearl" Shot
Reserved for celebrations (or just surviving a Monday), The Pearl is a chilled, creamy blend of white chocolate liqueur and coconut cream, served with a sugar-pearl rim. It’s sweet, smooth, and goes down easier than a summer Sunday.

How to Score the Secret Goods:
Our secret menu isn't about being fancy—it’s about being part of the family. These recipes are the ones our founders sip on when the sun goes down and the shutters go up.

Pro Tip: Mention you read the blog, and our bartenders might just show you a "hidden gem" of their own.

Come for the view. Stay for the secrets. See you at the shoreline! 🌊🐚
