About

Founded in 2010, The Drunken Mermaid is the salt-soaked brainchild of a powerhouse mother-daughter duo. Combining a mother’s eye for hospitality with a daughter’s flair for tropical mischief, they’ve spent over a decade proving that the best stories are told with sand between your toes and a drink in your hand.
